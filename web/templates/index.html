DualTalk Help

Quick start

Create or join a room, choose your role, enable camera, then hold each gesture steady in frame.

Privacy

Room context is cleared when the session ends. DualTalk does not store video or conversation history.

Better recognition

Use good lighting, keep your hand visible, and pause briefly between gestures.

Supported gestures

HELLO · YES · NO · HELP · WATER · FOOD · MEDICINE · PAIN · DOCTOR · THANK YOU · PLEASE · STOP · EMERGENCY
